# The Council Meeting

The group gathered around the weathered table in the Prancing Pony. Firelight flickered across their faces as they discussed their plan.

Barliman: "The roads have become dangerous of late. Travelers speak of strange shadows in the forest."

Gandalf: "We know of these dangers. The enemy stirs in the east."

Aragorn: "I will guard the forward path. No evil shall pass while I watch."

Frodo: "The ring weighs heavily upon me. I feel its pull growing stronger each day."

Barliman: "Perhaps some ale will steady your nerves, Master Baggins?"

Gandalf: "We must depart at first light. Time grows short."

Aragorn: "The shadow of Weathertop looms. We shall need all our courage."

Frodo: "I am afraid, but I will not falter. Not while my friends stand with me."

The hobbit looked around at his companions with renewed determination.

Gandalf: "That is the spirit we shall need. Together, we shall prevail."

Barliman: "May the blessings of the inn go with you. Return safely, friends."
